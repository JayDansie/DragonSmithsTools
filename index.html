Home

Welcome to Dragon Smith's Tools!

People have been playing Dungeons & Dragons for many decades.
This tabletop game has recently become more popular since the release of the Netflix original series "Stranger Things" and the newly released "Dungeons & Dragons: Honor Among Thieves" movie.

Let's be honest, D&D can be costly to start playing.
As more people take an interest in this game, it is only necessary that we make it more accessible and affordable for everyone.
That's where we come in!

Here we offer a few tools that can be helpful to any tabletop gamers.
Whether you are a new or experienced player, Dragon Smith's Tools has something for you!

Forgot your bag of dice? No worries!
Check out our dice roller!
Building a new character and you need some ideas?
Or are you hosting a campaign and you want to implement easy foraging?
We offer several generators that can help get you started!
Are you new to D&D and don't know where to start?
Check out some of our suggested videos about how to play D&D!

For more information about Dragon Smith's Tools, visit the About page.
